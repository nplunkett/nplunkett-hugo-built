This website is Nick’s personal blog containing his writing and archives.

Nick lives in Orange County, CA with his wife and son.

Nick is a seasoned Network Engineer and Peeirng Coordinator, and has deployed his skills across several industries including Research and Education and for-profit startups.

Nick returned to CENIC in 2023 as a Network Architect, in a group responsible for all aspects of design and deployment of the CalREN internet backbone for California.

Prior to this, Nick was the head of peering and network engineering responsbile for AS140627 at OneQode - an Australian low latency network and gaming first cloud computing company. He is responsible for building and tuning the network from layer 0 through 8 for this Asia Pacific based network.

Nick also worked as the main US peering coordinator, and anycast network stabilizer at AS32261: Subspace - a dedicated internet network for real-time applications backed by top VCs and talent from big tech.

Nick started his career at CENIC - California’s research and education network. There, he joined as a network operations intern and worked his way up in the company until he was eventually in charge of nearly all external network connectivity as CENIC’s Peering Coordinator.

Nick is at home when he is designing, building, and deploying world-class internet networks across the globe.
